The lab

A deliberately mixed group: surgeons, medical students, engineers, data scientists, and visiting scholars who work as collaborators, not assistants. Members publish, present at national meetings, and mentor the people who follow.

SB

Professor Stefano A. Bini, MD

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR
MARIA MANETTI SHREM ENDOWED PROFESSOR
CHIEF TECHNOLOGY OFFICER, UCSF ORTHOPAEDIC SURGERY

Dr. Bini replaces hips and knees, and has spent his career moving technology into surgical practice: operating-room robotics, machine learning on clinical and sensor data, and collaborations from Google to Milan. He founded DOCSF, the annual San Francisco conference where surgeons, engineers, and founders work out the digital future of musculoskeletal care.

He runs the lab on the same principle: surgeons, students, and engineers working together, with trainees given real ownership of their projects.
