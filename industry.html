What a partnership looks like

PILOTS & VALIDATION

Evaluate a technology

Put a device, platform, or algorithm in front of the surgeons who would use it, and measure it against defined clinical endpoints.

STUDIES & PUBLICATION

Answer a question with data

Bring a dataset or a clinical question. We design and run the study, from robotic intraoperative logs to federated record networks, and publish what we find.

ADVISORY & CO-DEVELOPMENT

Shape what's next

Anatomic insight, biomechanical modeling, and clinical-workflow knowledge, applied while a product is still being designed.
